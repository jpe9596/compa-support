## About Compa

Compa is your personal AI sobriety companion — always there when you need support, advice, or just someone to talk to.

### Features

- **AI Companions** — Jennifer and Bob are available 8am-10pm CST to listen, support, and guide you
- **Sobriety Tracker** — Track your clean time with beautiful visualizations
- **Meeting Finder** — Find AA and NA meetings near you
- **Craving Support** — Tools and guidance when urges hit
- **Daily Journal** — Document your recovery journey
- **Meditation & Prayer** — Calming content for spiritual wellness
- **Relaxing Music** — Soothing audio for difficult moments

### Privacy

Your privacy matters. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

- No account required
- Conversations stored locally on your device
- Location only used for finding nearby meetings
